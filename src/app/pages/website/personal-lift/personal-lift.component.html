Personal Pickup

From our stores, you can pick up orders placed online or buy best-selling perfumes and cosmetics at the same prices as on our website.

You can pay in cash or by credit card directly at the pickup point, and you can also use our stores for testing or directly purchasing top cosmetics and perfumes.

Our stores do not have their own stock, with your order being transported directly from the central warehouse in the Czech Republic. Before picking up your order, you will automatically receive a confirmation SMS when it is ready for pickup.

The delivery time for orders is between 2-4 working days from the date of shipment from the central warehouse.
